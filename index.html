bio

Anna Greenwood. 17 years old. Based in Central Serbia, in the heart of Europe. Tutored by Dr. Dragana Veljkovic, PhD in Music. Passionate about emotion in sound.

style

Agile, fluid, delicate. Elegant and soulful. Classical in technique, baroque in ornamentation. Ethereal in expression, volatile in emotion.

influnced by

Yiruma, Ludovico Einaudi, Hélène Grimaud, Lang Lang, Yuja Wang.

portfolio

Mozart, Bach, Beethoven, Haydn, Chopin, Tchaikovsky, Czerny, Handel.

aspirations

Whether through teaching or playing, Anna's passion is and always will be touching hearts with the beauty of music. She envisions herself composing on her own and teaching children the art of music in the future.
